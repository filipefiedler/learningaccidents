September 12, 2008 – Chatsworth train collision, California, United States: A northbound Metrolink double-deck commuter train runs a red light and collides head-on with a Union Pacific Railroad freight train pulled by three engines at about 60 mph and derails; the 220-ton derailed Metrolink engine is knocked 30 feet backwards into a 119-ton passenger car, crushing it in half. 
25 people are killed and about 135 are injured in the accident.
